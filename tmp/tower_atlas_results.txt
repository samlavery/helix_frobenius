(2) ROOT-NUMBER LAW OF THE TENSOR TOWER
==========================================================================
  eps(g) = eps_fin * i^{A(g)},  eps_fin = (+1)^{2^{g-1}} = +1  (g>=2),
  A(g) = sum_{p<g/2}(q-p+1)C(g,p) + [g even] C(g,g/2)/2
       = 2^{g-1} + g*C(g-1, floor((g-1)/2))   [closed form, proved =].
  A(g) is always EVEN (=> eps real, self-dual): 2^{g-1} even and
  g*C(g-1,.) even (central binomial even, or g even).  So eps in {+-1}.

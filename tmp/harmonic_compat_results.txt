  drives every leg at its own fundamental (w=1); the channels are integer
  combinations, conjugate-closed (B) at machine zero.  Three-way witness (C):
    w=1     : c=-1/2  -> MATCHES the motive's exact rationals (compatible)

                        the motive by >100 sigma (commensurate, wrong rung)

                        no motivic closure at all (incommensurate)
  The exact-rational atlas exists ONLY because every clock sits at its own
  fundamental on the shared commensurate lattice.  An incompatible clock
  would have produced a FALSE NULL / an un-recognisable transcendental --
  the compatible lattice is precisely what makes the tower read as a motive.

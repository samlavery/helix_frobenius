SENTENCE FOR THE PAPER:
Under the calibrated unclipped estimator (no clip; windowed projection at
parabolic-refined peaks; per-run calibration to 1.00000 on the exact truncated
explicit series), the Satake weight law holds at 1.1% for degree 2
(Delta k=1 median 1.000; E11 k=1 median 0.964 at 1.0%),
and -- at a height the degree-3 bank fully supports -- at ~0.2% for degree 3
(Sym^2 Delta k=1 median 1.001 at t<=500).  The old +-4% / 0.82+-0.13 figures were
method artifacts (dip-clipping plus an undersized bank), now retired.

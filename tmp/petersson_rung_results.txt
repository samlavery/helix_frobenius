STEP 1 -- THE CLASSICAL FORMULA (cited)
  Rankin-Selberg (Rankin 1939/Selberg; Kowalski notes; arXiv:1207.4336):
    D(w) = sum lambda(n)^2 n^-w = zeta(w) * A(w),  A(w)=sum lambda(m^2) m^-w
         = zeta(w) * L(Sym^2 Delta, w)/zeta(2w).
  Rankin residue (Rankin 1952 Proc.LMS, 'scalar product of modular forms'):
    Res_{s=12} sum |tau(n)|^2 n^-s = (3/pi)(4pi)^12/11! * <Delta,Delta>.
  => EDGE special value  L(Sym^2 Delta,1) = zeta(2)(3/pi)(4pi)^12/11! <Delta,Delta>.  (*)
  <Delta,Delta> = 1.035362056804320948e-6  (Zagier 1977; std SL2(Z) FD, dxdy/y^2).

  [verify] factorization D(w)=zeta(w)A(w) at w=2 (both converge):

    zeta(2)=1.64493406685  ratio-error=1.256e-5  [measured]
